# How to Add a Registrant to the Event

Go to the **Event** section   

Click on the **hamburger icon** beside the event in which you want to add a registrant. Select **View Event** from the menu. 

<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/deleteevent.png" alt="Servv" width="600" style="max-width: 100%; height: auto;" />

In the event details page, click on the **Registrants** section.  

<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/registrants2.png" alt="Servv" width="600" style="max-width: 100%; height: auto;" />

Fill in the registrant details:

- **First Name**
- **Last Name**
- **Email**

<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/registrants3.png" alt="Servv" width="600" style="max-width: 100%; height: auto;" />   

After filling all details click on **Add Registrant** to successfully add.

Click on **Save** to add the registrant successfully.

## View All Registrants
- Once you are in the **Registrants** section of the event, scroll below the form.  
- A list of **All Registrants** for that event will be displayed.  
- Each registrant entry includes the details you have saved, such as:
  - **First Name**
  - **Last Name**
  - **Email**  

<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/registrants4.png" alt="Servv" width="600" style="max-width: 100%; height: auto;" /> 

## Remove Registrant

In the list Select registrant which you want to remove then click on save.

<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/registrants5.png" alt="Servv" width="600" style="max-width: 100%; height: auto;" />
