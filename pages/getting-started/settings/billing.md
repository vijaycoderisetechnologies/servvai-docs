# Premium Plan Activation Guide

## Access the settings menu via the **Servv AI** sidebar. 
<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/settingsection.png" alt="Servv" width="200" style="max-width: 100%; height: auto;" />

## Navigate to the **Billing** section within the dashboard.  
<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/billing.png" alt="Servv" width="700" style="max-width: 100%; height: auto;" />

## Review the Servv Premium Plan details  
✨ **Try Premium — 7 days free, then $119.00 per year**  

## Select the **Activate** button located beneath the Premium section.  
<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/premiumplan.png" alt="Servv" width="400" style="max-width: 100%; height: auto;" />

## You’ll be redirected to the payment page, where you can complete the payment or start your free trial.

## Once the process is complete, your Premium plan will be activated automatically.
