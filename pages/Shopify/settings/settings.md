# Servv AI Settings Overview

The **Settings** section in Servv AI gives you full control over how your events are configured, displayed, and managed across your WordPress site. From setting global defaults to customizing booking flows and localization, these settings ensure a flexible and user-friendly experience for both organizers and attendees.

Explore the following key areas to tailor your event experience.

## General Settings
Set global defaults for your events to reduce repetitive setup and maintain consistency. Define:

- Default time zone and event duration
- Currency and pricing defaults
- Default event location

[Learn more →](https://support.servv.ai/getting-started/setting/general/)

This is your starting point for streamlined event creation.

## Reminder Settings
Ensure your attendees never miss an event. Set up automated email reminders to:

- Notify attendees hours or days in advance
- Customize timing and content of reminders
- Improve attendance rates with timely nudges

[Learn more →](https://support.servv.ai/getting-started/setting/reminder/)

## Billing Plans
Monetize your events with structured pricing. You can:

- Create multiple pricing tiers (e.g., Basic, Premium)
- Assign plans to different events or organizers
- Manage payment settings for a seamless checkout

[Learn more →](https://support.servv.ai/getting-started/setting/billing/)

## Fast Checkout
Simplify the user journey with a quick booking flow. This feature lets you:

- Enable fast booking from any device
- Customize checkout fields and labels
- Improve conversion rates for paid events

[Learn more →](https://support.servv.ai/getting-started/setting/fastcheckout/)

## Widget Settings
Control the look and behavior of embedded booking widgets. Easily:

- Adjust layout and style to match your brand
- Choose display types: calendar, list, or pop-up
- Configure behavior for specific pages

[Learn more →](https://support.servv.ai/getting-started/setting/widgetsetting/)

## Widget Translations 
Localize your booking experience for a global audience. With this setting, you can:
- Translate widget labels and messages
- Provide a multi-language experience
- Boost usability across international users

[Learn more →](https://support.servv.ai/getting-started/setting/widgettranslations/)
