# Servv AI Integrations Overview

**Servv AI** offers multiple integrations to help you connect your event management workflows with the tools you already use.  
This overview highlights the purpose and key benefits of each available integration.

## Email Integration
Easily connect your preferred email service to send automated notifications, confirmations, and reminders for events.  
Supported providers include Gmail, Outlook, and others.  
[Learn more →](https://support.Servv ai/getting-started/integration/email/)

**Key Benefits**
- Automated communication with participants.
- Branded and personalized email templates.
- Reliable delivery for important updates.

## Calendar Integration
Sync your Servv AI events with external calendars such as Google Calendar or Outlook. This ensures all team members and participants have up-to-date schedules without manual entry.  
[Learn more →](https://support.Servv ai/getting-started/integration/calendar/)

**Key Benefits**
- Real-time event syncing.
- Prevents scheduling conflicts.
- Easy access from any connected device.

## Zoom Integration
Link your Zoom account to Servv AI for seamless virtual event hosting. Event links are automatically created and shared with participants.  
[Learn more →](https://support.Servv ai/getting-started/integration/zoom/)

**Key Benefits**
- Instant Zoom meeting creation.
- Automatic distribution of join links.
- Simplified management of online sessions.

## Stripe Integration
Integrate Stripe to accept secure online payments for event registrations, tickets, or services directly through Servv AI.  
[Learn more →](https://support.Servv ai/getting-started/integration/stripe/)

**Key Benefits**
- Fast, secure payment processing.
- Support for multiple currencies.
- Automatic payment tracking in Servv AI.

## Why Integrate?
By connecting these tools with Servv AI, you can:
- Save time by automating repetitive tasks.
- Ensure accurate, up-to-date information across all platforms.
- Improve attendee experience with smooth communication, scheduling, and payment flows.
